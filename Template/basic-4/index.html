Who are we ?

We are a team of researcher in the Time and Frequency department in the National Institute of Metrologia (NIM). Our work consists of synchronising all atomics clocks from all over the world to a reference clock in real time using GPS time transfer, Real Time Remote Calibration (RTRC) and NIM Disciplined Oscillator system (NIMDO).
